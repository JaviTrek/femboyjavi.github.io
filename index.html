THE PROJECT
Forged Alliance Forever is a community-driven project designed to facilitate online play for Supreme Commander: Forged Alliance. Complete with chat, matchmaking, replay vaults, tournaments and ever evolving balance. If you want to experience Forged Alliance in its true glory together with thousands of other players of all skill levels, FAF is the place to be.
